About

The Creative Europe Project Results Platform is the European Commission’s database for the Erasmus+ Programme.

It contains descriptions of all projects funded under the programme, including contact information for the organisations involved.
It also contains results for projects that have ended, including links to websites. Success stories, or projects that have had exceptional results in terms of policy relevance, communication potential, impact or design, are highlighted on the platform.

They have been selected from a wider pool of good practice examples, or well-managed projects with very good results.

Why you should use it?

To enhance the visibility of your project
To share the results of your project with other people interested in the same topics
To find inspiration
To find partners for future projects

Complete list of all Creative Europe projects

Here you can find a list of all projects funded by the European Commission under the Creative Europe programme. You can save the list as an Excel file, so that you can apply your own filtering and sorting, and generate statistics about the programme.
