About

The cultural and audio-visual industries play a massive part in the everyday life and economic activity of millions of Europeans.
The Creative Europe dissemination platform helps you find inspiration from the hundreds of cultural and audio-visual projects already off the ground with EU support. This tool lets you explore existing projects, see what works by highlighting best practices and find the best projects around with our success stories.
